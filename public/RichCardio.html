RichCardio Website

RichCardio was my first website, created to sell medical balloons used for heart surgeries. The website was developed using HTML, CSS, PHP, and JavaScript, and it utilized XAMPP and SQL for database management. I worked as the web developer and the leader during my second year of college, and it was a team effort.

The website had the following key features:

  • Sign-Up and Login Functionality: Users could create an account, ensuring all fields were filled, emails and usernames were unique, and passwords matched. Validation was in place to avoid invalid characters or symbols. The login system validated users against stored credentials in the database.
  • Cart System: Users could view their added items, including the item name, price per unit, quantity, and total cost. They could remove items or adjust quantities before proceeding to checkout.
  • Purchase System: Users could enter their shipping address and complete the purchase through the website.
  • Log Out Functionality: The website offered a log-out option to securely exit the user session.

Unfortunately, the hosting website has been discontinued.
