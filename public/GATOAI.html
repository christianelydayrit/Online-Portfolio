AI GATO SUPPORT

I created an AI named Gato to practice and apply my learnings in AI automation. I used Voiceflow, Airtable, and Calendly. Gato can answer any questions related to me. You can book an appointment with me by simply asking or telling Gato that you want an appointment. This appointment will be recorded on my Calendly, so if an employer or someone finds this project interesting, they can book an appointment to discuss it. If you find an error or have feedback for me, just tell Gato that you want to leave a comment or message. Your information and the message will be stored on Airtable, which will help me acknowledge and read your message. If you're satisfied and done questioning Gato, you can say goodbye to Gato to end the session.

Features:

  • Gato can answer any questions related to Ely.
  • You can book an appointment with Ely by asking or telling Gato.
  • If you find an error or have feedback, you can tell Gato to leave a comment or message for Ely.
  • To end the session, simply say goodbye to Gato.
